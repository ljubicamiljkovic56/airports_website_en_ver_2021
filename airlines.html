Air Serbia

Air Serbia is the airlines company from Serbia. The company's headquarters are located in Belgrade, Serbia, and the main hub is Nikola Tesla Airport. This company has recently cooperated with the Air Asia airlines, which means that passengers from Serbia will be able to travel safely and comfortably to Singapore, Japan, China or Malaysia.

Air Asia

Air Asia is a Malaysian low cost airline based near Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. It is the largest airline in Malaysia in terms of fleet size and destinations. Air Asia Group operates scheduled flights at home and abroad to more than 165 destinations in 25 countries. Now it also transports passengers from Serbia to various Asian destinations at affordable prices.

Bamboo Airways

Bamboo Airways is a low-cost airline. Founded in 2017, this airline started operating on January 16, 2019. The airline operates a mixed fleet of narrow-body and wide-body aircraft and has hubs at Noi Bai International Airport and Tan Son Nhat International Airport. Starting of 2021, Air Service also offers Bamboo Airways flights.
